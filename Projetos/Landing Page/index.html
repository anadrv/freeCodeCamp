XDINARY HEROES

Xdinary Heroes (엑스디너리 히어로즈 / eecs/su/di/nou/ri-hee/eo/ro/su) is a six-member, South Korean idol rock band under JYP Entertainment's sub-label, StudioJ. Xdinary Heroes debuted on December 6, 2021, with the digital single Happy Death Day.The band's name is an abbreviation of "Extra-Ordinary Heroes", meaning "Anybody Can Become A Hero".

Starting on November 22, JYP Entertainment began releasing performance videos that revealed each member's role in the band. Jooyeon appeared playing the bass, followed by O.de on the synthesizer, Gaon on electric guitar, Jun Han also on electric guitar, Jungsu on the keyboard, and finally Gunil on the drums.
